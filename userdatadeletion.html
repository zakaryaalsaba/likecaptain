How to Request Data Deletion

At Like Captain, we respect your privacy and provide you with the option to delete your data. Follow the steps below to request the deletion of your data:

  • Log in to your Like Captain account.
  • Go to the "Settings" section of the app.
  • Select the "Delete Account" option.
  • Confirm your request by following the on-screen instructions.

Manual Data Deletion Request

If you are unable to delete your data through the app, you can request data deletion manually:

  • Send an email to support@likecaptain.com.
  • Include the subject line: "Data Deletion Request".
  • Provide your account details (e.g., registered email address) for verification.

We will process your request within 7 business days.

Data Retention Policy

We may retain certain data for legal or security purposes as required by applicable laws. After the retention period, all data will be permanently deleted.
